Capture Parking

Bachelor's Project, Mumbai University

Developed a parking monitoring System used to determine the availability of parking spaces in organized and unorganized parking lots. Collected historical data that helped to predict real time availability of parking at any given point of time and determined which areas can be converted into parking spaces using data mining techniques. Obtained the images from the CCTV at the parking and determined the availability of the parking and notify the users about the parking. Provided a system based on Mask R-CNN for analyzing the images to get the information about the parking Published a technical paper on this project in S-JPSET Journal 20 May 2020 Vol.12 Issue1 (ISSN-2454-5767)
